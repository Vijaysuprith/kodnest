ISKCON Rajarajeshwari Nagar (RR Nagar)

The ISKCON centre in Rajarajeshwari Nagar, located near BEML Layout in southwest Bengaluru, serves as a spiritual sanctuary for devotees and visitors. This temple, part of the wider ISKCON Bangalore family, upholds the mission of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness. Devotees gather to chant the Hare Krishna maha-mantra, celebrate festivals, and engage in bhakti programs inspired by the teachings of Lord Krishna and Srila Prabhupada.
